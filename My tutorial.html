HOW TO CREATE A GMAIL ACCOUNT

There are 6 steps to create an Gamil account

Step 1: Access to Google. Then, click on Gmail at the top right corner at the page.

Step 2: Create an account. Click on the Create an account button at the top right corner.

Step 3: Set-up. To set up your new gmail account, Google need your information. First, your first and last name. The ‘choose your username’ is the unique email address that you wish to use, which will be placed before ‘@gmail.com’.After you get the user name you wish, then you will come up with a password for your account. And the last thing, are some detail information about yourself such as birthday, gender, phone number, and your location.

Step 4: Verification. To get to the next steps you will need to Accept Google's privacy and terms first, once you have read it click on I Agree.

Google will ask you to verify your account via text or voice call, select option you want then click on Continue

If you choose the text option to verify your account, you will see the new window where you have to enter the code what Google sent to your phone numer. After you get the code, click on Continue and now you have created your Gmail account.

Step 5: Creating a profile picture. You can set up a photo to show as your profile picture. To set up your profile picture click on the profile icon on the top right hand corner of the page and click Change.

Step 6: Completing your set up. You can complete your Google mail setup by clicking the available resources on the dashboard.
